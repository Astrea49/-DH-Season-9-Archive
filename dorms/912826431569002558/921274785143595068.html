Lou-Mari Asencio BOT 2021-12-17 08:51:46Z
Today has been rather eventful. The discovery of a body is always something unpleasant no matter how much experience Lou has with them. The loss of a life, it's never something to simply ignore. Lou can't act as if it doesn't matter, because it does. While it isn't her responsibility whatsoever, she feels like running into some people to see how they're feeling. The psychological landscape has changed so much within the past twenty-four hour time period. She can't help but feel like this is a prime opportunity to psychoanalyze whoever she runs into, even if she's already spoken to them. A chance to learn, is a chance to learn. At the current moment, Lou is fast asleep on her bed. She went to bed thinking of the excitement that is the recent event. She's used to seeing the dead, but she's not used to how things proceed with this game of death everyone is playing. The feeling of the unknown, for some reason she loves it. It's something akin to the dark dealings that she would frequently expose in the media. The affects of such doings, she never cared about until it finally came back to haunt her, claiming the life of a loved one. A loved one she couldn't ever get out of her head because of the massive cloud of regret that surrounds her at every turn of her life.

She doesn't turn, hoping the feeling goes away, but sometimes what you want isn't always what you get. Despite not moving initially, she slowly turns to see what may be there. This feeling is almost never wrong with her. And the feeling didn't betray her. As soon as Lou turns, she's standing face to face with a familiar being. Not a ghost of her past, but rather a soul she left behind, even if purely accidental. Standing in the shade, back to the tree facing the blondie. This figure has pale blonde, almost pink-ish hair.
