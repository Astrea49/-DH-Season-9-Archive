Another key characteristic of the house is that it is upside-down. Balancing only through the front steps and the roof, the house looks completely unnatural. Still though, it persists. A large clock sits to the side, telling time with absolute precision.

"If you stare at the abyss for long enough, it will stare back at you." There's also a collection of scrolls and book in here for the avid scholar. All of the books have something to do with demonology, witchcraft, or something else on the subject. The covers on the books reflect that. However, one of the books stands out. Unlike the other books, it's bright green with wooden tones and bound by vines. Strange.
Six desks and six beds are also in this house. For the house of magic, the bedframes and desks are made of that mysterious black substance. The desks contain pencils and sheets of paper. There's a small trash can as well. The four beds are evenly spaced throughout the dorm, each bed with its own desk and lamp, as well as wardrobe that holds clothes. Each desk and wardrobe is locked, and must be unlocked with a Monopad. These locks and the locks on the front door are the only semblance of technology in this place. A bathroom is also linked to here, containing a rustic toilet along with a somewhat working shower. The door does lock, and it's supposed to accommodate for all members of this house. Basic amenities are provided for everyone, despite the house being not so modern. For example, a 21st century sink, bath/shower, and toilet are provided with items like soap and toothbrushes. However, items like a hose from the 19th century that they used to wash out bathrooms are here. A razor from the 17th century seems dull and unsafe to use. Overall, you should be able to use the area.
