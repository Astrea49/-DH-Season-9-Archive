Small wisps of blue flame lead you to this ruined temple, remade into the trial room. The flames lead you deep into the forest, a section that if you were to try looking for yourself, you would probably get lost looking for. At the foot of the steps, you stand before a large stone structure, what once was a temple to an unknown god. Vines and moss are growing around the structure, and torches suddenly go ablaze as you walk towards it. It's almost like the place comes to life as you walk in. With every step that you take up into the trial room, another torch gets lit, leading you deeper into the structure. No one can be seen lighting these torches, but yet they are. Once you finally arrive in the room, there are 22 stone pedestals all arranged in a circle around one huge bonfire. The bonfire isn't tall enough to obscure your view of the other side, though. Towards the back of the room is a stone throne, reserved for Monokuma. It's too small to fit anyone else, after all. To call this place a room is a bit inaccurate. The 'room' is more of an open area, due to the crippled nature of the walls and the lack of a roof. There's nothing stopping you from walking straight off the edge of the place in some areas. However, the room is open directly up into the sky, where you can see the stars. The torches around you gives the whole area a tribal sense. Ready? It's time for the trial.
